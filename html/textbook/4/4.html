Words / Expressions

Directions : Listen and repeat after your teacher.

driver's permit a permit which allows a person that is learning to drive
#I sit shotgun I want to sit in the front passenger seat. To sit shotgun' is to sit beside the driver in the front passenger seat of a car.
freak someone who is extremely interested in a particular subject so that other people think they are strange or unusual
roundabout a raised circular area where three or more roads join together and which cars must drive around
intersection a place where roads, lines etc cross each other, especially where two roads meet
counterclockwise in the opposite direction in which the hands of a clock move

Short Conversation

Directions : Read the following conversation with your teacher.

Ken got a driver's permit and now he's practicing driving a car with Aidan and Charlotte.
Aidan So you got a driver's permit, which is good.
Tell me how long have you been driving a car?
Ken Not long, actually. In Japan, I have a driver's license, but no real practice.
Aidan In that case, I need to urge you to drive properly, so I sit shotgun.
Ken That's good to hear. Have you found out what you're having yet?
Aidan 'Shotgun' means 'sitting in the passenger seat'.
So 'I sit shotgun' means 'I sit in the passenger seat'.
Charlotte In that case, I will sit in the back and tip you off when I see something dangerous. But Ken, you should keep your eyes on the road, too, please.
Ken Yes, ma'am. I'm not a speed freak or anything, so trust me on that.
