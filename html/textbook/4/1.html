Words / Expressions

Directions : Listen and repeat after your teacher.

folks parents
pump to make liquid or gas move in a particular direction, using a pump
self-serve gas station gas station where you pump your own gas.
obnoxious very offensive, unpleasant, or rude
gouge to charge excessive prices for something / to make a deep hole or cut in the surface of something
shortage a situation in which there is not enough of something that people need

Short Conversation

Directions : Read the following conversation with your teacher.

Layla is driving her car while talking to Ken who is sitting in the passenger seat.
Layla So, how is your homestaying coming along?
Ken Oh, it's great! I'm blessed to have an American family like you guys.
Layla Yeah, yeah. My folks are good people, but whenever you decide to move out, you know you're more than welcome to stay with us.
Ken Thanks, I'll remember that. Hey, Layla, could you drop me off at the library in the campus?
Layla Sure, no problem. But, let me pull into the nearest gas station and fill up the tank because I'm getting low on gas.
Ken Ok, I'm not in a hurry anyway.
Oh and I want you to show me how to pump gas there.
Layla What do you mean? You have never pumped gas yourself?
Ken Nah, never. There weren't many self-serve gas stations back in my hometown in Japan.
