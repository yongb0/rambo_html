Advanced Lesson 15

Bed time

Phrasal Verbs

turn off / leave on / wake up / doze off

turn off
to stop a machine or an appliance from working by using a button or a switch
leave on
to keep a machine or an appliance on
wake up
to become conscious again after sleeping, or to make someone else become conscious after sleeping
doze off
to go to sleep, especially when you did not intend to

David and Layla are having a short conversation before their bed time.
