Middle Lesson 47

I should have checked my phone sooner.

Today's GOAL

To be able to express regret.
後悔を表現できるようになる。

Today's Phrase

I should have checked my phone sooner.
You could have talked to her in person.

Past Modals ( Should have Vpp / Could have Vpp )

"Should (not) have"...You made a mistake or didn't do something that was a good idea.
I should have checked my phone sooner. (= She regrets not checking her phone.)
"Could (not) have"...You didn't do it although it was a possibility at the time.
She could have talked to Dr. Doyle. (= She missed a chance to talk to Dr. Doyle.)

Exercise

Directions: Make sentences using should have Vpp / shouldn't have Vpp.

Example: Albert failed the exam because he didn't take notes during the class.
>> He should have taken notes during the class.
  1. Linda got a stomachache because she ate a gallon of ice cream.
    ______________________________.
    She shouldn't have eaten so much ice cream
  2. Ben got in trouble because he insulted the pianist.
    ______________________________.
    He shouldn't have insulted the pianist.
  3. Victoria can't go to the park because she didn't do her homework.
    ______________________________.
    She should have done her homework.
  4. Paul is embarrassed because he forgot Gloria's birthday.
    ______________________________.
    He shouldn't have forgotten her birthday.
  5. The Browns are not feeling well today because they went to bed late last night.
    ______________________________.
    They shouldn't have stayed up so late.

Exercise

Directions: Complete the sentences using could have Vpp / couldn't have Vpp.

Example: Luke didn't get in a fight with his coworker.
He could have called their boss.
  1. We didn't have to work for the *worthless company, __________.
    (*worthless = くだらない)
    We could have worked for another company instead.
  2. They didn't have to get the loan from the ABC bank, __________.
    They could have gone to another bank.
  3. He didn't have to sleep on the uncomfortable couch, __________.
    He could have slept on the bed.
  4. I didn't have to get my hair cut at Mod's hair salon, __________.
    I could have gone somewhere else.
  5. She didn't have to apologize to me for what happened, __________.
    she could have acted like nothing had happened.
