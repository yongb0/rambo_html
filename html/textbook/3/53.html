The gate's been broken for a long time.

Today's GOAL

To be able to master the passive voice in all its forms.
あらゆる種類の受動態をマスターできるようになる。

Today's Phrase

The gate has been broken for a long time.
It should be repaired by the next spring.
The Passive Voice ( be ＋ Vpp )
I am injured. / He will be fired next month. / Coffee was served after dinner.
It has been broken. / The meeting might be delayed.
The phone should be fixed. She must be mistaken. / They could be forgotten.

Short Conversation

Directions : Read the following conversation with your teacher.

Emily's parents took a road trip and they're visiting Emily now.
Emily Dad, how was your journey to California?
Father It was great. Your mother and I drove through Arizona and Nevada to see *Hoover Dam. It was built in 1936, did you know that?
Emily No, I didn't, but I've seen it before. It is magnificent, isn't it?
Father Yes, indeed. On our way here, there was a lot of construction work. That's one of the reasons why our trip was delayed. I guess the government is spending a lot of our tax money on the maintenance of the transportation network. I'm not quite sure it's worth it though.
Emily Yeah, I guess most organizations are that way.
Many schools in this country are repairing buildings for no reason.
Father Are you talking about the gate at your school?
Emily Oh, that gate has been broken for a long time, although nobody ever had any problem with it before. I wonder why it's taking forever to fix it. I hope it'll be repaired by spring.

*Hoover Dam once known as Boulder Dam, is a concrete arch-gravity dam in the Black Canyon of the Colorado River on the border between the U.S. states of Arizona and Nevada.

Exercise

Directions: Change the sentences into their the passive forms.

Example:
Construction work on the highway delayed their arrival for about a day.
Their arrival was delayed for about a day by construction work on the highway.

  1. The government should *provide more money for a better educational system.
    (*provide = 提供する)
    More money should be provided by the government for a better educational system.
  2. James has *posted some of his pictures on SNS (social networking service.)
    Some of James' pictures have been posted by him on SNS.
  3. They gave the *candidates three minutes to answer the questions.
    (*candidate = 候補者)
    The candidates were given three minutes to answer the questions.
  4. People have twice *elected Bill Clinton to be the President of the United States.
    (*elect = 選ぶ)
    Bill Clinton has twice been elected to be the President of the United States.
  5. They might choose Dr. Alba to be the next head of the science department.
    Dr. Alba might be chosen to be the next head of the science department.
  6. Some children broke the backdoor a long time ago.
    The backdoor was broken a long time ago by some children.
