Short Conversation

Directions: Read the following conversation with your teacher.

Samantha has been acting strange and Charlotte finally got to talk to her.
Charlotte So where have you been hiding from all of us?
Samantha I needed some space from everyone and everything to heal myself.
Charlotte Heal yourself of what?
Samantha Stupid thoughts and possibility that I might possibly like James.
I know it's funny.
Charlotte It's not funny. Actually, I knew it. I mean I did catch slight signs as soon as you started acting all strange. Then I drove to your apartment, but by the time I got there, you had already vanished.
Samantha I know! The moment he told me about his new girlfriend or whoever, I dropped my phone and the screen got smashed so easily! Like it was a sign or something!
Charlotte How are you feeling now?
Samantha Feeling better. It was much more shocking than I could ever imagine, though. Not the fact that he's seeing an attractive girl, but the fact that I actually like him.
