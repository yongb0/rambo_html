Middle Lesson 56

He lost your stuff not once but several times.

Today's GOAL

To be able to master some expressions with "NOT" and use them comfortably.
"NOT" を使ったいくつかの表現をマスターし、不自由なく使えるようになる。

Today's Phrases

He told me not only to design the buildings but to give a presentation to the clients.
He lost my stuff not once but several times.

Not only A but also B

My boss told me to design buildings. My boss also told me to give a presentation.
>>My boss told me not only to design buildings but also to give a presentation.
Not A but B
His assistant didn't lose his stuff once. He lost it several times.
>>His assistant lost his stuff not once but several times.

Exercise

Directions: Complete the sentences using not only A but also B.

Example : He told me not only to design buildings, but also to give a presentation to the clients.

  1. Hey, those chocolates are ____________________. (for you / for your brother)
    not only for you, but also for your brother.
  2. My friend asked ____________________. ( to throw a party / to bake a cake)
    not only to throw a party, but also to bake a cake.
  3. The famous actor ___________________ . (directed the movie / starred in it. )
    not only directed the movie, but also starred in it.
  4. They are __________________________________ . ( intelligent / creative )
    not only intelligent, but also creative.
  5. His doctor told him __________________ . ( to quit smoking / to exercise more)
    not only to quit smoking, but also to exercise more.

Exercise

Directions: Change the sentences using not A but B.

Example : He lost your stuff not once, but several times. He should be fired by now.

  1. My girlfriend ___________________ . ( give me orders / give me self-confidence)
    doesn't give me orders, but gives me self-confidence.
  2. He is ______________. ( the most handsome man / the most trustworthy man)
    not the most handsome man, but he is the most trustworthy man.
  3. They did __________________. ( give up / worked hard for the rest of the week)
    not give up, but worked hard for the rest of the week.
  4. He betrayed ________________________________. ( once / a few times)
    you not only once, but a few times.
  5. We could ________________. ( get angry in public / cried when we came home)
    not get angry in public, but cried when we came home.
