Adverbial Clause ( Like / The way / As ) ～のように・～の通り

"like / the way / as" are used to talk about ways and the conditions.
Ex : My parents were against it as we expected.
I'm doing it just the way the recipe says.
Like I always say, you guys have many things in common.
