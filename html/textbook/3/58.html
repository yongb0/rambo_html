Short Conversation

Directions: Read the following conversation with your teacher.

Right after Emily came home from work, Aidan's sister, Leah, knocked on the door.
Emily Hey Leah, I haven't seen you in ages. Is everything alright? Come on in.
Leah Is this a bad time? I've got great news and this visit was a total impulse. I hope I'm not inconveniencing you.
Emily No, not at all. You look happy. What is your news?
Leah Yeah. The human resources people at JS Airline called and yes I got a job!!
Emily Oh my god. Really? Congratulations! I was actually a bit concerned about your well-being, since you looked a little down after the job interview.
Leah Right, I was really down. As a flight-attendant candidate, I was rejected for not being tall enough, but I came to be able to be an airline staff. I can work at the airport, so I'm happy.
Emily That's great! You must be really lucky because not everyone gets such opportunity like that. Have you told your parents about it?
