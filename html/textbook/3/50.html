Middle Lesson 50

Did he tell you that you'd have to come?

Today's GOAL

To be able to change direct speech into reported speech.
直接話法を間接話法に変更できるようになる。

Today's Phrase

He said he'd been calling your cellphone.
Did he tell you that you'd have to come?
Direct Speech vs. Reported Speech
Direct speech: He says to me “I love you.” He said to me “I hate you.”
Direct speech: He says (that) he loves me. He said (that) he hated me.
> When you change direct speech into reported speech, you have to maintain a constant verb tense.

Short Conversation

Directions : Read the following conversation with your teacher.

Benjamin's son called him at his law firm while he was busy having a meeting.
Benjamin What did my son say?'
Secretary He said he had been calling your cellphone to discuss the budget.
Benjamin Budget!? The last time I talked to him, he said he had declined the timely offer to work for his uncle's enterprise. I wonder what budget he's talking about after all that. Did he say anything about the document?
Secretary Yes, Sir. He said he had already shipped a package including his birth certificate as you told him. Oh! And he also mentioned that he would be in town next week so he would like to have lunch with us.
Benjamin Us? Who is "us" ?
Secretary Sir somehow, I was invited, too.
Benjamin Let me guess, he told you that you would have to come and join us in order to make the discussion peaceful and smooth like butter, didn't he?
Secretary Not that exactly, but it might have been something like that.

Exercise

Directions: Change the sentences using reported speech.

Ex : The secretary said to Ben, “Your son called. He is going to be in town next week.” The secretary told Ben (that) his son had called. He was going to be in town next week.
  1. Linda said to Paul, "I've never been in love."
    Linda told Paul.
    that she'd never been in love.
  2. Sarah said to Emma, "Your room looks messy, you will have to clean it."
    Sarah told Emma.
    that her room looked messy, and that she would have to clean it.
  3. My husband said to me, "You will feel better when you've had something to eat." .
    My husband told me that I would feel better when I'd have something to eat.
