Short Conversation

Directions : Read the following conversation with your teacher.

Sarah has been trying to call Olivia for the past half an hour but the line has been busy.
Sarah Hey, who were you talking to? The line has been busy for a long time, Mom.
Olivia I was talking to my aunt, Mary. She told me her brother-in-law had died last night.
Sarah You mean uncle Jack? What happened?
Olivia It seems like he had a stroke.
Sarah It must have happened recently because I talked to him on the phone a few weeks ago.
Olivia Yeah. It was a pretty sudden death. It must have caused a lot of grief for the family though.
Sarah Are you going to attend his wake and funeral?
Olivia Yes, I think I'm going. I just wish I could have visited him while he was still alive.
Sarah I guess it's normal to mourn and regret like that, Mom. Did you ask if they were doing okay?

Exercise

Directions: Complete the sentences using would have, should have, could have, must have, might have.

  1. Ever since his brother died, he hasn't been eating well, .
    he must have lost his appetite.
  2. I wish I had gone to the party last night so that .
    we could have had fun together.
  3. I didn't learn anything from the lecture, .
    I should have paid more attention.
  4. Tom is not here yet, .
    he might have been delayed.
  5. If he had acted like that in front of my friends, .
    he would have gotten in trouble.

Exercise

Directions: Change the sentences using reported speech.

  1. Luke said to his wife "Don't get mad at me."
    Luke told his wife not to get mad at him.
  2. James asked Andrew "Have you seen Samantha?"
    James asked Andrew if he'd seen Samantha.
  3. My teacher asked me "How did you do on the exam?"
    My teacher asked me how I had done on the exam.
  4. Olivia said to Daniel "You will feel better when you've had something to drink."
    Olivia told Daniel he would feel better when he'd had something to drink.
  5. Leah asked Aidan "Where can I get a cellphone like yours?"
    Leah asked Aidan where she could get a cellphone like his.
  6. David asked Ryan "What have you been doing?"
    David asked Ryan what he had been doing.
