Middle Lesson 51

He asked me how you were doing.

Today's GOAL

To be able to change direct speech into reported speech.
直接話法を間接話法に変更できるようになる。

Today's Phrase

He asked me how you were doing.
They told me not to tell you anything about it.
Direct Speech vs. Reported Speech
Direct speech! He said to me "Don't talk." He said to me "How are you?"
Reported speech! He told me not to talk. He asked me how I was.

> When you change direct speech into reported speech,you have to maintain the constant verb tense.

Short Conversation

Directions : Read the following conversation with your teacher.

Daniel came back from his brother-in-law Ben's house in the evening.
Olivia How did the yard work at Ben's house go?
Daniel We cut down some trees and dragged them back with my tractor.

I think we're done for today.

Ben asked me how you were doing. How was your day?
Olivia It was good. Our daughters were here.
Daniel Oh really? How were they doing?
Olivia They were doing great. They asked me if Naoko's brother was really coming to the states, and if he was, when would that be.
Daniel Have they talked to my brother?
How did they know about Naoko's brother?
Olivia I don't know. They told me not to tell you anything so I'm gonna have to let you speculate all night. Hahaha.
Daniel Uh-oh, someone is being cagey today.
Olivia That's right. Good secret-keepers never reveal their sources.
Daniel Hahaha is that so? I'll have to find some bait to get you to talk about it.

Exercise

Directions : Directions: Change the sentences using reported speech.

Example:
My mother said to me "Don't talk to strangers".
My mother told me not to talk to strangers.

  1. Lisa said to her daughter "Don't play in the street." >>__________________ .
    Lisa told her daughter not to play on the street.
  2. Tim said to his wife "Don't tell me what to do." >>__________________ .
    Tim told his wife not to tell him what to do.
  3. The suspect said to the police "Don't shoot me." >>__________________ .
    The suspect told the police not to shoot him.
  4. David told Layla "Don't wait for me." >>__________________ .
    David told Layla not to wait for him.
