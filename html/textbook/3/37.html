Middle Lesson 37

My parents were against it as we expected.

Today's GOAL

To be able to talk about ways and the conditions.
方法・状況について話せるようになる。

Today's Phrases

My parents were against it as we expected.
I'm doing it just the way the recipe says.

Adverbial Clause ( Like / The way / As ) ～のように・～の通り

"like / the way / as" are used to talk about ways and the conditions.
Ex : My parents were against it as we expected.
I'm doing it just the way the recipe says.
Like I always say, you guys have many things in common.

Short Conversation

Directions: Read the following conversation with your teacher.

Layla walks in while Emily is struggling to bake some pastries (= breads / sweets).
Layla Hey, how are you doing here?
Emily Oh it's a disaster.
I'm about to destroy the entire kitchen or something, although I really am doing it just the way the recipe says.
Layla Hahaha. I believe you. Are you using unsalted butter?
Emily No, I'm not because it says "butter" here on the book. See?
Layla Yeah, but it's a basic rule though.
When you make pastries, you use unsalted butter.
Emily Oh god! How do you know that?
Anyway, how was the family dinner last night?
Layla It was a disaster, too.
Charlotte started saying she wanted to go to the Middle East to visit Allison, and my parents were against it as we expected.
Emily Hahahaha. Like I always say, you and Charlotte have so many things in common.
Layla How? Charlotte resembles me because we are sisters, but that's all.

Exercise

Directions: Complete the sentences using as, like or the way.

Answers have to be ALMOST exactly the same.

Example : If you ever have a chance, you as we expected.. ( as : 予想通り)

  1. Please don't complain and just do ___________________ . ( as : 言われた通り)
    as you are told.
  2. _____________ , you two have so many things in common. ( like : 言ったように)
    Like I said
  3. It’s your wedding, so you should organize things _______ . ( the way : したいように)
    the way you want to.
  4. The food didn't come out right, although I cooked it___________ .( as : 本の通り)
    as the book said.
  5. His daughter grew up and became a politician _________ . ( like : 彼が言ったように)
    like he said.

Exercise

Directions: Complete the sentences using How dare ～ ! ＋ the given words.

Answers don't have to be exactly the same.

Example : How dare you propose to me after proposing to my sister last month! ( propose / after).

  1. How dare you ________________ ! ( charge … for ～ : ～の代金として…を請求する)
    charge a fee for the broken rental car which didn't even work!
  2. How dare he _________________________! ( steal … from ～ : ～から…を盗む )
    steal money from my bank account!
  3. How dare you ___________________! ( demand … from ～ : …を～から要求する )
    demand more holidays from the company!
  4. How dare she ________________________________!( hire : 雇う / without )
    hire that rude guy without my permission!
  5. How dare you __________________________! ( behave : 振舞う / in front of )
    behave like that in front of my family!
