Middle Lesson 57

I came to know them better as I lived with them.

Today's GOAL

To be able to talk about what happens over a long period of time.
長期にわたって起こることについて表現できるようになる。

Today's Phrases

I came to know them better as I lived with them.
Getting to know other cultures is also learning about your own culture.

Come to ～ / Get to ～（= become to do ～）

I didn't know much about them, but now I know them because I live with them.
>> I came to know them better as I lived with them.
>> I got to know them better as I lived with them.

Short Conversation

Directions: Read the following conversation with your teacher.

Charlotte went to visit Allison who temporarily lives in the Middle East.
Charlotte So how is everything? You really look great, Allison.
Allison I'm doing well. It was a bit difficult in the beginning due to my broken Arabic, like I couldn't pronounce some words correctly and therefore, I couldn't communicate with people. But now that they're familiar with my broken Arabic, everything is good.
Charlotte Yeah, I was amazed by how welcoming they are! The staff at the airport were kind, too. I like their hospitality
Allison True, that's one of the things I love about Arabic culture.
I came to know them better as I lived with them, and now I really feel grateful to be here.
Charlotte Oh, I'm sure! You seem to be well adjusted to your new environment.
Allison Getting to know another culture is also learning about your own.
Charlotte, you are like my little sister, so let me tell you this : If you ever have a chance to go and see the world, take it! I wouldn't say the younger the better, but having the experience itself is what matters.

Exercise

Directions: Make the sentences using 'come to' ～ /get to ～ ＋ given words ＋ as（ ～につれて）

Answers don't have to be exactly the same.
Example : Allison came to know them as she lived with them for a while.
(come to / know)
  1. He ______________________________________ . ( come to / appreciate )
    came to appreciate his parents more as he grew older.
  2. She ______________________________________. ( get to / like )
    got to like Biology as she took more classes.
  3. We ______________________________________. ( come to / be friendly )
    came to be friendly with Tom as we hung out with him.
  4. They ______________________________________. ( get to / understand )
    got to understand the materials as they studied hard.
  5. I ______________________________________. ( come to / feel lonely )
    came to feel lonely as I lived in my room all by myself.

Exercise

Directions: Change the sentences using had better.

Answers don't have to be exactly the same.

Example : If you ever have a chance, you had better travel around and see the world.

  1. Prices of most things might be more expensive there, I __________________.
    had better bring more money to NY.
  2. If you haven't gone anywhere yet this winter, _____________________.
    you had better go somewhere and enjoy the holidays.
  3. They didn't make it on time last time, they_____________________.
    had better leave their apartment earlier this time.
  4. If you didn't follow today's lecture, you _________________________.
    had better go and talk to the professor after class.
  5. He didn't take his umbrella with him. We _________________________.
    had better go and pick him up.
