Beginner Lesson 47

Where is the nearest hotel from here?

ここから、最も近いホテルはどこですか？

Today's GOAL

To be able to compare things and choose the superlative thing among many things.
物事を比較し、多くの物の中から最高の物を選べるようになる。

Today's Phrases

Where is the nearest hotel?
最も近いホテルはどこですか？
Which is faster, a cab or a bus?
どちらがより速いですか？タクシーですか、それともバスですか？
A cab is probably faster, but a bus is cheaper.
タクシーがおそらくより速いですが、バスはより安いです。

Comparative （ 比較級）-er
Ex : fast >> faster / cheap >> cheaper / good >> better / cute >> cuter /busy >> busier / tall >> taller

Superlative （ 最上級）the -est
Ex : fast >> the fastest / cheap >> the cheapest / good >> the best /cute >> the cutest / busy >> the busiest

↑The form of an adjective that has the strongest meaning. For words of 1 or 2 syllables, add '–est' . For words or 3 or more syllables, say 'most' before it. (i.e. The Biggest Loser. The most amazing thing I've ever seen.)
