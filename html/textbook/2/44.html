I'm afraid not
残念ながら違うね
This is a polite way of delivering bad news. The statement must be bad news, but must not be your fault.
Ex. I'm afraid you have cancer.

If it IS your fault, say "I'm sorry (statement)."
Ex. I'm sorry I lost your phone.
