Beginner Lesson 03

Do you like movies?

映画は好きですか？

Today's GOAL

To be able to talk about things you like and things you don't like, things you want and things you don't want.
好きな物や好きではない物、欲しい物や欲しくない物について話す事が出来るようになる。

Short Conversation

Directions : Read the following conversation with your teacher.
講師と一緒にストーリーを読んでみましょう。

Layla is spending the night at the apartment of her friend, Allison.
レイラが友達のアリソンのアパートに泊まっています。
Allison Layla, do you like movies?
(レイラ、映画好き？)
Layla Yeah, I do.
(うん、好きよ。)
Allison What kind of movies do you like? I have some DVDs.
(どんな種類の映画が好き？いくつかDVD持ってるんだけど。)
Layla What do you have?
(どんなのを持ってるの？)
Allison I have drama, comedy, horror, documentary films and suspense films.
(ドラマでしょ、コメディー、ホラー、ドキュメンタリー、それからサスペンスもあるわよ。)
Layla I like horror movies.
(ホラー映画が好きよ。)
Allison Great! I like them, too.
(良かった！私もホラー映画好きなの。)
Layla Hey, do you want anything while watching the movie?
(ねぇ、映画を見ながら何か食べたい？)
Allison I want some popcorn and coke.
(ポップコーンとコーラが欲しいな。)
Layla Sounds good
(良いわね！)

Example(例)
Teacher Do they like movies?
You Yes, they do.
Teacher What kind of movies do they like?
You They like comedy movies, but they don't like horror movies.

【 like >> comedy / don't like >> horror 】

Teacher Do they want any food?
You Yes, they do.
Teacher What kind of food do they want?
You They want Italian food, but they don't want Japanese food.

【 want >> Italian food / don't want >> Japanese food 】
