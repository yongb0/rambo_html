Beginner Lesson 54

You are lying to me,aren't you?

あなたは嘘を言ってるんですよね？

Today's GOAL

To be able to express certainty or uncertainty using tag questions with "to be".
be 動詞の付加疑問文を使って、確信またはあいまいさを表現できるようになる。

Today's Phrases

You are lying to me, aren't you?
あなたは嘘を言ってるんですよね？
You are not serious, are you?
あなたは本気じゃないよね？
*For this lesson, we are only teaching them tag questions with "to be".
We will cover other types of tag questions in the next lesson. (55)

Tag Question（ 付加疑問文）

>> when you are certain and you want someone to agree with you.
(確かなことへの同意を求める）

Ex: It's a beautiful day, isn't it? (今日は良いお天気ですね？（そう思いませんか？））

>> when you are uncertain and you want to find out about it.
（ あいまいな事を確かめる）

Ex: You are not serious, are you? (まさか本気じゃないですよね？）
