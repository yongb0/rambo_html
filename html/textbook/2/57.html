New Words / Expressions

Directions: Listen and repeat after your teacher.

on the spot
すぐその場で・ただちに
*Without prior warning
If someone invites you to something 'on the spot', you don't have enough time to make an
excuse and may be forced to accept even if you don't want to.
I got invited to the dinner on the spot tonight.
held
hold（開く・催す）の過去分詞
Where was the dinner meeting held?
built
build（建てる）の過去分詞
The building was built in 1950, wasn't it?
drunk
酔っ払った
Are you drunk?
vintage (old; antique)
上質の・ビンテージものの
A lot of vintage wine made me drunk.
fix

Directions: Look at the following pictures and make a tag question with the passive form.
次の写真を見て、受動態の付加疑問文を作りましょう。

The passive（受動態） >> be ＋ Vpp （ 過去分詞） or get ＋ Vpp （過去分詞）

【例: Example】
Teacher He gave her a diamond ring today.
You She was surprised, wasn't she? or
She got surprised, didn't she?
Teacher They are having so much fun at the club now.
You ?

You ?
( 彼は、おびえていましたよね？）
He was frightened, wasn't he?
Teacher Craig and Anna finally had their first date.
You ?
　( 彼らは、ワクワクしていましたよね？）

Exercise

Directions: Read the following conversations.
Fill in the blanks with "make", "have", "let", or "get".
次の会話を読んで、"make", "have", "let", または "get" を使って穴埋めをしましょう。

  1. Mark : I'm sorry, but I don't think I can fix the toilet.
    Katie : It's okay. I will _____ the maintenance man to come and fix it.
    get
  2. Olivia : Why did you go to the meeting tonight?
    Daniel : I didn't have a choice. My boss _____ me go.
    made
  3. Angelina : Would you please tell Oscar to call me back tonight?
    Ken : Sure, I will _____ him know.
    let
  4. Ms. Harris : Where is the document from the client? I need it tomorrow.
    Michael : Yes, sir. I will _____ my assistant bring it to you this afternoon.
    have
  5. Liz : I can’t finish packing our stuff. I think we should _____ my brothers to help us.
    get
    Samara : Are you sure? I don't want to bother them.
