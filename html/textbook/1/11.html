Short Conversation

Directions : Read the following conversation with your teacher.
講師と一緒にストーリーを読んでみましょう。

Charlotte is sick, and talking to her mother Olivia, in bed.
シャーロットは体調が悪く、ベッドで母親のオリビアと話しています。
Olivia How are you doing, sweetheart?
(気分はどう？)
Charlotte Oh I'm resting, I'm so sick. What time is it now?
(休んでるわ、とても具合が悪いの。今何時？)
Olivia It's 11 o'clock.
(11時よ。)
Charlotte Mom, please call Pavel and tell him the lab meeting is at 2 p.m. today.
(お母さん、パーベルに電話して、研究室の会議が今日の午後2時だと伝えて。)
Olivia Pavel? Who is he and where is he from?
(パーベル？彼は誰でどこの出身なの？)
Charlotte He is my lab partner, from Russia. He's a smart guy.
(彼は私の研究室のパートナーでロシアから来たの。とても頭がいいの。)
Olivia I see. That's nice. What's his number?
(なるほど。すばらしいわね。彼の番号は何番？)
Charlotte 257-6081.
(257-6081よ。)
Olivia Anything else? Are you hungry?
(他に何かある？お腹空いてる？)

Charlotte I'm not hungry, but I am thirsty.
(お腹は減ってないわ、でも喉が乾いてるの。)
Please give me water and medicine.
(水と薬をちょうだい。)
Olivia Alrignt. Stay calm, okay?
(わかったわ。安静にしてるのよ？)
This is quite unnatural since one only says 'Stay calm' if someone else is panicking, but Charlotte is very calm in the conversation.
