Entry Lesson 12

Whose umbrella is this?

これは誰の傘ですか？

Today's GOAL

To be able to ask and answer questions about who the owner of something is.
誰の物であるか聞いたり答えたり出来るようになる。

Today's Phrase

Whose umbrella is this?
これは誰の傘ですか？
It's my sister's umbrella.
それは妹の傘です。

If she's talking about only one of her brothers (who has a twin), it would be: My twin brothers' guitar.
If the guitar belongs to both of the twins (as in the story): My twin brothers' guitar.
