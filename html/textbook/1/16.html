Entry Lesson 16

How many months are there in a year?

1 年にいくつの月がありますか？

Today's GOAL

To be able to ask about quantitative amounts.
数量について質問できるようになる。

Today's Phrase

How many months are there in a year?
1年は何か月ですか？
How many questions do you have?
質問はいくつありますか？

Short Conversation

Directions: Read the following conversation with your teacher.
講師と一緒にストーリーを読んでみましょう。

The little boy William, is talking to his father in the car.
ウィリアムが、車中で父と話をしています。
Father What are you learning in school these days, Willy?
（最近学校で何を学んでるんだい、ウィリー？）
William We are learning numbers.
（僕たちは、数を勉強してるよ。）
Father Okay, I have a quick quiz for you then. How many months are there in a year ?
（そうか、君のためにクイズをしよう。1年は何か月かな？）
William There are twelve months in a year. Easy! How many questions do you have?
（1年は、12ヶ月だよ。簡単だね！質問はいくつあるの？）
Father I have one more question. How many minutes are there in an hour ?
（もう1つ質問するよ。1時間は、何分かな？）
William There are sixty minutes in an hour. See?
（1時間は、60分だよ。どうだい？
I like numbers, Dad. I'm good at it.
僕は数が好きなんだよ、パパ。得意なんだ。）
Father Yes, I see that, Willy. You are a smart kid. Here we are.
（そうだな、わかったよ。君は賢い子だ。さぁ、着いた。）
Have a nice day at school, son!
（学校でよい1日を、息子よ!）

Exercise

Directions: Be familiar with months and weeks.
次の写真を見て、質問したり答えたりしてみましょう。

【Example : 例】
Teacher How many months are there in a year?
You There are twelve months in a year.
(1年は、12ヶ月あります。)
Teacher How many days are there in November?
You There are thirty days in November.
(11月は、30日あります。)
You How many days are there in a week?.
(1週間は、何日ありますか？)
Teacher There are seven days in a week.
Teacher How many days are there in a year?
You There are three hundred sixty five days in a year.
(1年は、365日あります。)
Teacher How many hours are there in a day?
You There are twenty four hours in a day.
(1日は、24時間です。)

Teacher How many boxes do they have?
You They have five boxes.
(彼らは、5つの箱をもっています。)
You How many bags do they have?.
(彼らは、いくつの袋をもっていますか？)
Teacher They have seven bags.

Free Conversation

Directions:Let's use the phrases and the words you've learned during the lesson and talk about the following topic freely!
レッスン中に習ったフレーズや単語を使って自由に話してみましょう！


Talk about things that belong to you or your teacher by using "How many". 『How many~?』を使って、あなたの持ち物や講師の持ち物について話してみましょう。
Ex:
How many people are there in your family? There are six people in my family.
How many keys do you have? I have four keys.
