Entry Lesson 15

Are there any cars on the street?

通りに何台か車はありますか？

Today's GOAL

To be able to talk about if there is or there are thing(s) in a certain place or not.
ある特定の場所に物があるかどうか話すことが出来るようになる。

Today's Phrase

Are there any cars on the street?
通りに何台か車はありますか？
Yes, there are some cars on the street.
はい、通りに何台かの車があります。

Short Conversation

Directions: Read the following conversation with your teacher.
講師と一緒にストーリーを読んでみましょう。

Samantha is talking to Callum, the exchange student from New Zealand.
サマンサがニュージーランドからの交換留学生カラムと話をしています。
Samantha Tell me about your hometown, Callum.
（あなたの故郷について話して、カラム。）
Callum It's normal. There are many sheep on the farm in my hometown.
（普通だよ。僕の故郷には沢山の羊が牧場にいるよ。）

There are also many farms for cows and pigs.
（牛や豚のための牧場もたくさんあるんだ。）
Samantha That's nice, but not normal for me. Are there any cars on the street?
（素敵ね、でも私にとっては普通じゃないわ。通りにいくつか車はある？）
Callum Yes, there are many cars in the cities, but not in my hometown.
（うん、都会には沢山の車があるよ、でも僕の故郷はそうでもないね。）
Samantha Is there a big shopping mall around your house?
（あなたの家の周りに大きなショッピングモールはある？
Callum No, there isn't. There are small stores near my house.
（ないね。小さなお店とかなら家の近くにあるよ。）
Samantha I get it! Are there any dogs for the sheep then ?
（わかったわ！じゃぁ羊たちのための犬はいる？）
Callum Of course there are! Now we're talking!
（もちろん、いるよ！そうこなくっちゃ。）

Question

Directions: Answer the following questions about the story.
ストーリーに関する質問に答えてみましょう。

  1. Are there any cars on the street in Callum's hometown?
  2. Is there a big shopping mall around Callum's hometown?
  3. Are there any cats for the sheep on the farm?

Free Conversation

Directions: Now ask your teacher about her / his hometown by using "Is / Are there～?".
『Is there～? Are there～?』を使って講師の故郷のことを尋ねてみましょう。

Have the students ask you at least a few questions about your hometown (ex : Are there any cafés near your office?) and you answer them using "There is/are" (ex : Yes there are. There are small cafes near my office.)
